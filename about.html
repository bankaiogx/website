WHAT IS GYMXP?

GYMXP is a gamified fitness experience designed to make training addictive, rewarding, and fun. Every rep, set, and workout earns XP that contributes to your personal level.

As you progress, you unlock new Aura Modes—visual power-ups that represent your improvement, from Ember Mode at the start to Phoenix Mode as you rise. With AI-driven trainers who adapt to your style, GYMXP guides you through personalised routines built for growth.

Compete with friends through leaderboards, challenges, and streaks, and make every achievement feel meaningful. GYMXP transforms your fitness journey into a motivating, game-like experience that delivers real-world results.
